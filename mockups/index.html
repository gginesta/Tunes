How to pick

  1. Open all three in separate tabs and flip between them.
  2. Imagine a noisy living-room with friends passing a phone around — which screen sets the right tone?
  3. Reply with your pick (A / B / C), or a mix ("B's glass + A's vinyl cards"). Full concepts are combinable.

Once you pick, I'll build the design system (tokens + components) and re-skin the whole app: Home, Lobby, Game, Results, Rules, Leaderboard, Profile, and the waiting-state trivia moment.
