Direction A — Vinyl & Neon

Deep purple with tri-color neon (magenta · cyan · amber). Vinyl-disc centerpieces, record-sleeve timeline cards, chunky display type. A disco / record-shop feel — loud, musical, unmistakably a party game.

Design notes

  • Hero is a vinyl record. Home, lobby, and the in-game mystery card all use the same rotating record motif, reinforcing the brand every time you look at the screen.
  • Tri-color neon (pink · cyan · amber) replaces the mono-green palette. Pink = primary action, cyan = secondary / info, amber = tokens and warnings — no more color confusion.
  • Sleeve cards give the timeline physical presence: each card is an album sleeve with a peeking record, decade year in chunky "Bowlby One", and color per era.
  • Unbounded for headlines, Space Grotesk for body. Less "black-weight everything", more rhythm.
  • Ambient glow + scanlines add atmosphere — the app feels like a dim club, not a dashboard.
