Direction B — Modern Glass

Aurora gradients, real glassmorphism, sophisticated type (Fraunces + Inter). Softer gradient palette (mint · plum · peach) instead of pure Spotify-green. Reads like a premium music app — Apple Music + Arc browser energy.

Design notes

  • Aurora-per-turn. The background subtly shifts color based on the current track's decade or mood — the environment itself reacts to the music.
  • Real glass. Every panel is frosted glass with proper backdrop-blur, a thin inset highlight, and a subtle shadow — premium depth without clutter.
  • Serif + sans. Fraunces for headlines (with occasional italic-serif flourishes) adds elegance that a pure-sans app can't match. Inter keeps body text clean.
  • Gradient CTAs. Plum→mint gradient replaces the mono-green pills; still bright, but no longer screams "Spotify clone".
  • Album-as-hero. Mystery card is a circular album with a waveform badge — echoes Apple Music's Now Playing but adapted for the guessing moment.
