Direction E

Arcade Cabinet

Pixel typography, CRT scanlines, 3D arcade buttons, coin-op token UI. Leans into "Tunes is a GAME" — high scores, power-ups, and boss-fight energy.

Design notes

  • CRT scanlines overlay every screen — subtle but sets the retro-game tone instantly.
  • HP bars for scores. Card progress is a health-bar fill, making the race to 10 feel competitive and visible.
  • Pixel typography (Press Start 2P) for headings + labels gives instant arcade recognition.
  • 3D arcade buttons with drop-shadows that compress on press — satisfying tactile feel.
  • Per-player neon colors (pink · green · blue · yellow) read like character select in a fighting game.
