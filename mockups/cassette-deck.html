Direction D — Cassette Deck

Warm analog tones, cassette tape as hero, VU meters, tape-deck transport controls. Nostalgic hi-fi hardware — the perfect vibe for a music game about decades past.

Design notes

  • Cassette as centerpiece. Home and mystery card are physical cassettes with spinning reels — unmistakably "music" and deeply nostalgic.
  • VU meters replace generic equalizers — every beat visualized like real hi-fi gear.
  • Warm analog palette. Brown/beige/amber instead of cold blue-black. Orange + mint accents feel like vintage electronics (Sony Walkman, Panasonic decks).
  • Monospace everywhere. Share Tech Mono gives a dashboard/readout feel — like reading a tape counter.
  • Mini-cassette cards. Each timeline card is a tiny cassette with spinning reels — the whole timeline is a mixtape shelf.
